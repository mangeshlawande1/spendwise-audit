## FAQ

**Q: Is this actually free?**
A: Yes. We show you the audit results before asking for your email. No credit card, no trial, no catch.

**Q: How do you know the pricing data is accurate?**
A: Every number in the audit engine traces back to an official vendor pricing page URL, verified the week of launch. See our PRICING_DATA.md for full citations.

**Q: What does Credex get out of this?**
A: If we find significant savings, we'll mention that Credex sells discounted AI infrastructure credits. That's it. The tool is genuinely useful whether or not you ever buy anything.

**Q: My spend is custom/negotiated. Will this work?**
A: Enter what you actually pay per month — the audit engine uses your real spend, not catalog prices, as the baseline.

**Q: Is my data private?**
A: Your email and company name are never shown on the shareable URL. Only the tools and savings numbers are public.
